Private Credit Boom
Private credit has grown into a $2T+ market, replacing banks as the primary lenders to mid-market companies. PE firms (Apollo, Ares, Blackstone) now control lending that banks once dominated. This shifts credit risk off regulated balance sheets and into less transparent structures. Understanding this is essential for any credit or leveraged finance role — it's one of the most significant structural changes in finance this decade.
